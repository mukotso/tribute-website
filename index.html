Sterwart Butterfield

C.E.O and Co-founder Slack

Background

Daniel Stewart Butterfield (born Dharma Jeremy Butterfield;March 21, 1973) is a Canadian billionaire businessman, best known for co-founding the photo-sharing website and the team-messaging application slack.

Butterfield was educated at St. Michael's University School in Victoria British Colombia and made money in university designing websites. 

He received a B.A. degree in philosophy from the  University of Victoria in 1996 and went on to earn a Masters of Philosophy from Clare College Cambridge in 1998.
